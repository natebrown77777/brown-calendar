Privacy Policy

Brown Calendar · Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Brown Calendar ("the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Summary

The App is designed to keep your information private. All data you create in the App — your tasks, notes, and settings — is stored only on your own device. The App does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information with us or with any third party. We do not operate any server that receives your data, and we have no access to anything you store in the App.

Information the App Stores

The App stores the following information locally on your device so that it is available the next time you open the App:

  • Tasks you create, including their names, repeat settings, and start/stop dates.
  • Notes you write for individual days.
  • Your preferences and the current state of the App.

This information is kept in your device's local application storage. It is not transmitted to us and is not accessible to us.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any of the following: your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal or device information. The App contains no advertising, no analytics or tracking tools, and no third-party data-collection software.

Device Backup

Your device's operating system may automatically include the App's data in the standard system backup provided by your device (for example, Android's built-in backup feature). This backup, if enabled, is handled entirely by your device and your own Google account — it is controlled by your device settings, governed by Google's privacy policy, and is not something we operate, access, or receive. You can turn this backup on or off in your device's system settings. The App also lets you manually export a backup file of your tasks and notes; where you choose to save or send that file is entirely up to you.

Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not send your data over the internet to us. Any standard network capability is used only by your device's operating system for routine functions such as the optional system backup described above.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because no information is collected or transmitted, the App is suitable for users of all ages.

Data Security

Because your information never leaves your device (except through a backup or export you control), its security depends primarily on the security of your own device. We recommend protecting your device with a passcode or other lock. We cannot access your data and therefore cannot recover it if your device is lost or reset without a backup.

Your Choices

You are in full control of your data. You can delete tasks and notes within the App at any time, and uninstalling the App removes its locally stored data from your device. You can manage or disable system backups through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected in an updated version of the App where appropriate.
